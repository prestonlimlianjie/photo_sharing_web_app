########### Story 1 ####################
Story number and name: Story 1, Photo Likes
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action:
This is similar to Facebook's like button - users can hit the like button associated with their own or other users' photos.
Users can see the number of likes that each photo has.


########### Story 2 ####################
Story number and name: Story 2, Favorites
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action:
Users can 'favorite' photos on the site; all photos favorited by a user will be displayed in their favorites page.
On the favorites page, users have access to thumbnails of the photos that they have favorited, and can click on the thumbnails
to enlarge the photo and check out the date they favorited the photo, and the date the photo was created.

########### Story 3 ####################
Story number and name: Story 3, New Activity Updates
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action:
Users can see what their and other users' most recent activity on the site is. These activities are defined as:
Commenting, Uploading a photo, Logging in, Logging out, and Creating new user.
